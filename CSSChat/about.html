About AIPCA St. Peters Muguga Church

Our History

AIPCA St. Peters Muguga Church started at Cheerful Child and has grown to become a cornerstone in the community.

After gathering in different locations, the church was blessed to purchase its own land in Machine, Nakuru, where we continue to serve God and the community.

Our mission is to bring people together in faith and fellowship, creating a supportive environment for all.
